# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

B2B Industrial Solutions actively maintains and provides security updates for the current production version of **b2bindustrial.in**.

| Version                      | Supported |
| ---------------------------- | --------- |
| Current live version         | ✅ Yes     |
| Previous versions            | ❌ No      |
| Development or test versions | ❌ No      |

Visitors should always use the latest publicly available version of the website.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

B2B Industrial Solutions takes the security of its website, users, and business information seriously.

To report a suspected security vulnerability affecting **b2bindustrial.in**, please email:

**[sandeep@b2bindustrial.in](mailto:sandeep@b2bindustrial.in)**

Include the following information where possible:

* A clear description of the vulnerability
* The affected page or URL
* Steps required to reproduce the issue
* Screenshots, logs, or proof-of-concept information
* The potential security impact
* Your name and preferred contact details

Please do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until B2B Industrial Solutions has investigated and resolved the issue.

## Response Timeline

After receiving a valid security report, we will aim to:

* Acknowledge receipt within **3 business days**
* Provide an initial assessment within **7 business days**
* Share progress updates when there is a significant development
* Resolve confirmed vulnerabilities as quickly as reasonably possible

The time required to resolve an issue may depend on its severity, complexity, and potential impact.

## Accepted Reports

If the vulnerability is accepted, we will:

* Confirm that the issue is valid
* Assess its severity and potential impact
* Begin remediation
* Inform the reporter when the issue has been resolved
* Credit the reporter where appropriate and with their permission

We do not currently offer a paid bug-bounty programme unless agreed in writing before testing.

## Declined Reports

A report may be declined when:

* The issue cannot be reproduced
* The reported behaviour does not create a meaningful security risk
* The issue has already been reported or resolved
* The report concerns an unsupported third-party service
* The testing violated this policy or applicable law

Where possible, we will provide a brief explanation for declining the report.

## Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

Security researchers must:

* Act in good faith
* Access only the information necessary to demonstrate the vulnerability
* Avoid accessing, modifying, deleting, or downloading personal or confidential data
* Avoid disrupting the website or its services
* Avoid denial-of-service testing
* Avoid social engineering, phishing, or physical-security testing
* Avoid automated testing that generates excessive traffic
* Give us reasonable time to investigate and fix the issue before disclosure
* Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

## Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not authorised:

* Denial-of-service or distributed denial-of-service attacks
* Malware distribution
* Phishing or impersonation
* Credential stuffing or brute-force attacks
* Accessing another person’s account or information
* Modifying or deleting website data
* Disrupting website availability
* Testing third-party systems without permission
* Publicly disclosing unresolved vulnerabilities

## Safe Harbour

B2B Industrial Solutions will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

* Conduct testing in good faith
* Follow this security policy
* Avoid privacy violations and service disruption
* Promptly report discovered vulnerabilities
* Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate them

This safe-harbour statement does not apply to illegal, harmful, fraudulent, or intentionally disruptive activities.

## Scope

This policy applies to:

* **b2bindustrial.in**
* Official subdomains operated by B2B Industrial Solutions
* Website functionality directly controlled by B2B Industrial Solutions

Third-party services, hosting providers, social-media platforms, payment providers, and external websites are outside the scope of this policy unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## Policy Updates

B2B Industrial Solutions may update this security policy periodically. The latest version published on **b2bindustrial.in** will apply.
