Statutory Compliance

Statutory compliance refers to the legal framework within which an industrial entity must operate. This includes adherence to labor laws, environmental regulations (like DPCC/CPCB), and safety standards (like the Factories Act). At B2B Industrial, we manage the end-to-end documentation and site audits required for these approvals.

The renewal period depends on the category (Red, Orange, or Green) assigned by the State Pollution Control Board. Generally, Red category industries require renewal every 5 years, while Green can be up to 15 years. We help track these deadlines to ensure zero operational downtime.

Energy Audits

A Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) certified audit identifies specific areas of energy leakage. Benefits include up to 20% reduction in utility costs, improved equipment lifespan, and lower carbon footprints, helping your company meet ESG targets.

HVAC & Duct Cleaning

Robotic cleaning uses specialized camera-mounted crawlers that reach deep into complex duct networks where human entry is impossible. It ensures 100% dust removal, improves air quality, and prevents fire hazards caused by greasy buildup.

HSE & Fire Safety

Our Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) audits cover physical safety (fire exits, signage), chemical management, PPE compliance, and emergency evacuation protocols. We ensure your facility meets both Indian standards and international safety benchmarks.
