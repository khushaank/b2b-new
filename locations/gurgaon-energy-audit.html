Relevant Indian & International Standards

We ensure that our energy assessments stand up to rigorous scrutiny:

  • Energy Conservation Act, 2001: Fully compliant with the national mandates outlined by the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE).
  • Haryana State Specific Code: Aligned with the Haryana Energy Conservation Building Code (HECBC) where applicable.
  • ISO 50001:2018: International guidelines for the setup and execution of Energy Management Systems.
  • ISO 9001:2015: Ensuring rigorous quality control in our own surveying and reporting processes.

Our Proven 7-Step Process

1. Site & Scope Evaluation: Initial walkthrough of your Gurgaon facility to understand the single-line diagram and critical load centers.
2. Live Measurement: Strategic placement of NABL calibrated instruments to record real-time data on chillers, AHUs, compressors, and motor control centers over an appropriate duty cycle.
3. Precision Analytics: Identifying leakage in compressed air, inadequate insulation, and motor mismatches.
4. Energy Balancing: Comparing utility bills with recorded sub-meter data to ensure full accountability of energy flows.
5. Cost-Benefit Matrix: Generating viable ECOs (Energy Conservation Opportunities) tailored to the local material availability in Gurugram, plotting out the direct ROI.
6. Actionable Reporting: Presenting the BEE certified energy audit report with crystal clear managerial data.
7. Turnkey Execution: Optional end-to-end execution of the recommended retrofits by our in-house engineering team to guarantee the projected savings.

Case Study: Gurgaon Auto-Ancillary Plant

Challenge: A major tier-1 automotive supplier located in IMT Manesar, Gurugram faced unexpectedly high electricity bills despite consistent production volumes.

B2B Industrial Solution: We conducted an intensive 7-day electrical and thermal audit. Discovered severe losses in the compressed air network (over 28% leakage rate) and identified three massive ID fans operating via dampers instead of VFDs.

Results Delivered:

  • Executed leak-arrests reducing compressor specific energy consumption drastically.
  • Installed dynamic VFDs, yielding an immediate drop in motor amp draw by over 35%.
  • Overall energy consumption reduced by 17.5%, yielding a staggering ROI of exactly 8.5 months on the capital invested.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Can you audit a facility located in Udyog Vihar or Manesar quickly?

Absolutely. Headquartered in Gurgaon at Khandsa Road, we offer rapid mobilization to IMT Manesar, Udyog Vihar, Sector 37, and Dharuhera clusters.

What are the common findings in Gurgaon's corporate IT parks?

In corporate IT hubs, HVAC typically accounts for 60% of energy bills. We frequently identify unoptimized chillers and a lack of VFDs on chilled water pumps as primary areas for immediate savings.

Will the energy audit interrupt our ongoing production?

No. Our engineers use non-invasive, clamp-on meters to capture data while your machines run at normal operational loads. No shutdowns are required for the audit phase.
