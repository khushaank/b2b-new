What is an ESG Audit in Gurgaon?

ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) auditing is a process of evaluating a company's impact on the environment and society, along with its governance structures. In Gurgaon's thriving corporate sector, ESG audits are becoming essential for attracting investment, improving brand reputation, and ensuring long-term sustainability.
