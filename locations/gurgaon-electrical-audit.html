What is an Electrical Safety Audit in Gurgaon?

As the primary industrial belt surrounding Delhi, Gurugram (Gurgaon) encompasses sprawling automotive suppliers in IMT Manesar and dense IT infrastructure across Cyber City. Why Your Gurgaon Industry Needs It Now

With massive heat waves and rapidly fluctuating grid voltages common in the Haryana industrial zones, aging or stressed electrical equipment is a ticking time bomb.

  • Prevent Local Disasters: Short circuits remain the #1 cause of industrial fires in the NCR. Thermal scanning detects these hidden, melting joints weeks before ignition.
  • HVPNL & CEIG Compliance: Comply strictly with Haryana Vidyut Prasaran Nigam Limited (HVPNL) and the Chief Electrical Inspector to Government’s mandatory safety provisions.
  • Secure Massive Data Centers: For IT parks, any flicker or MCCB trip results in data loss. Our predictive maintenance guarantees uptime.

Relevant Indian & International Standards

We ensure that your Gurgaon facility passes both domestic and global scrutiny:

  • CEA Regulations 2010: National foundational safety measures.
  • National Building Code (NBC) 2016: Ensuring fire-safe electrical routing.
  • IS 3043: Strict adherence to Indian grounding and earthing protocols to protect CNCs and Robotics.

Our Proven 7-Step Process

1. Scope Verification: Checking existing Single Line Diagrams against physical plant modifications.
2. Physical Clearances: Ensuring HT/LT rooms satisfy minimum statutory working clearances.
3. Thermography Scanning: Using Fluke/FLIR imagers to spot overheating contactors in running panels without shutting down the factory.
4. Earthing Verification: Driving earth-tester rods to test specific resistance on sensitive utility equipment.
5. Relay Calibration Checking: Verifying tripping parameters across main incomers.
6. Comprehensive Reporting: Providing a risk-categorization matrix highlighting immediate fire or shock hazards.
7. Direct Resolution: Deploying our team to execute re-cabling, lug replacement, or busbar balancing within 24 hours.

Case Study: Manesar Auto-Parts Forging Unit

Challenge: Continual nuisance tripping of an 800-ton forging press creating massive raw material waste.

B2B Industrial Solution: Deployed thermography and power quality tracking. Discovered 5th and 7th order harmonics were feeding back into the LT panel, causing the neutral to hit 115°C.

Results Delivered: Arrested an imminent phase-fire, re-lugged the core inputs, installed an active harmonic filter. Operations returned to 100% capacity with zero trips.
