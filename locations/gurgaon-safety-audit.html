What is a Safety Audit in Gurgaon?

A safety audit is a management tool for evaluating the health and safety performance of an industrial unit. In the manufacturing and logistics hubs of Gurgaon, safety audits are conducted to identify hazards, assess risks, and ensure compliance with the Factories Act and IS 14489 standards.

Key Components of Our Safety Audit

  • Hazard Identification (HIRA): Systematic identification of workplace hazards.
  • Machine Safety: Auditing machine guarding and interlocking systems.
  • Chemical Safety: Reviewing MSDS and storage practices for hazardous materials.
  • Fire & Electrical Safety: Integrated checks for fire prevention and electrical hazards.
  • PPE Compliance: Evaluating the adequacy and usage of Personal Protective Equipment.

Why IS 14489 Compliance is Vital

IS 14489 is the Indian Standard for Occupational Health and Safety Auditing. Adhering to this standard ensures that your facility is following a recognized framework for safety management, which is often a requirement for corporate clients and regulatory bodies.
