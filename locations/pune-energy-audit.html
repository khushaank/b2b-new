Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is a BEE audit mandatory for Bhosari MIDC units?

Any unit consuming more than the threshold specified by the State Designated Agency (MAHURJA) or BEE must undergo a legal audit. We provide BEE Accredited reports for these requirements.

How can Pune's forging units reduce power costs?

Induction heating optimization and power quality (harmonic) analysis are the two most effective methods for forging units to reduce their electricity bills.
