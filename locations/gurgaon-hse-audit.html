What is an HSE Audit in Gurgaon?

An HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) audit in Gurgaon is a systematic evaluation of an organization's compliance with safety regulations and environmental standards. In the industrial landscape of Gurugram and Manesar, HSE audits are critical for ensuring worker well-being, minimizing environmental impact, and avoiding legal penalties.

B2B Industrial Solutions provides specialized HSE auditing services that cover occupational health, industrial safety, and environmental management systems (ISO 14001 & ISO 45001).

Key Benefits of HSE Audits

  • Regulatory Compliance: Stay ahead of state and central safety regulations.
  • Accident Prevention: Identify hazards and implement controls to reduce workplace injuries.
  • Environmental Protection: Optimize waste management and emission controls.
  • Operational Efficiency: Improve productivity by ensuring a safe and healthy workforce.

Our HSE Audit Framework

1. Scope Definition: Aligning audit objectives with your specific industry needs.
2. Hazard Identification: Detailed walkthrough to identify physical, chemical, and biological hazards.
3. Compliance Check: Verifying adherence to Factories Act, PCB norms, and ISO standards.
4. Performance Review: Evaluating the effectiveness of existing safety programs.
