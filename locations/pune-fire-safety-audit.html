Built for Pune operations

Pune supports automotive plants, engineering units, IT campuses, pharmaceutical facilities and supplier parks. These sites face mixed process utilities, compressed-air networks and changing production shifts. A useful fire & life safety therefore needs more than a generic checklist: it must reflect how the facility actually runs, where access is restricted and which actions can be completed without disrupting production.

B2B Industrial Solutions plans fieldwork around operating conditions, gathers traceable evidence and presents findings in a format that engineering, EHS, finance and leadership teams can use together. The objective is life safety, emergency readiness and maintainable fire protection—with clear action ownership rather than a report that sits unread.

What the Pune assessment covers

The final scope is confirmed after a short discovery call and document review. A typical engagement includes:

01

Hazard and egress review

Occupancy hazards, escape routes, exits, signage and assembly arrangements.

02

Detection and alarm

Detector coverage, alarm audibility, panels, interfaces and testing records.

03

Firefighting systems

Hydrants, sprinklers, pumps, extinguishers, water storage and access.

04

Emergency readiness

Roles, drills, impairment control, training and corrective-action follow-up.

A four-stage route from site visit to closeout

Define

Confirm objectives, site boundaries, operating shifts, records and access constraints.

Inspect

Review documents, interview responsible teams and assess live site conditions.

Prioritise

Grade findings by risk, urgency, evidence, effort and operational impact.

Close

Discuss actions, ownership and proof required to demonstrate completion.

Questions facility teams ask

How long does a fire & life safety take in Pune?

Duration depends on area, process complexity, shifts and measurement scope. A focused single facility may need one or two field days; multi-block or multi-shift sites are planned in phases after document review.

Can the assessment happen while the facility is operating?

Yes, much of the review is more useful under representative operating load. Any intrusive test or isolation is agreed in advance and carried out only under the facility’s authorised safety procedure.

Does the report include implementation support?

Yes. Findings are discussed with responsible teams, and B2B Industrial Solutions can support scope definition, vendor coordination, engineering checks and closure verification where requested.
