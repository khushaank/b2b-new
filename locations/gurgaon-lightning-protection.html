What is a Lightning Protection Audit in Gurgaon?

A Lightning Protection System (LPS) audit is a specialized evaluation of a building's lightning protection and earthing systems. In Gurgaon, where seasonal thunderstorms can be severe, ensuring that your facility is protected according to IS/IEC 62305 standards is critical for preventing fire, equipment damage, and loss of life.

B2B Industrial Solutions conducts detailed lightning protection audits in Gurgaon to verify the integrity of air terminals, down conductors, and earth termination systems.

Our Lightning Protection Audit Scope

  • Risk Assessment: Calculating the lightning risk based on location and building structure.
  • Physical Inspection: Checking for corrosion and continuity in the LPS network.
  • Earth Resistance Testing: Measuring earth pit resistance to ensure low-impedance paths.
  • Surge Protection (SPD): Verifying the installation and health of surge protection devices.
  • IS/IEC 62305 Compliance: Ensuring the entire system meets national and international standards.

Why LPS Audits are Mandatory for High-Rise Gurgaon

With Gurgaon's skyline dominated by high-rise corporate towers and industrial chimneys, the probability of a lightning strike is significantly higher. A regular audit ensures that the protective systems installed at the time of construction are still functional and effective.
