Why Your Bangalore Industry Needs It Now

With Bangalore’s rapidly expanding industrial footprint and increasing power loads on the BESCOM grid, aging or stressed electrical equipment poses serious risks to your operations.

  • Prevent Electrical Fires: Short circuits and loose connections remain the leading cause of industrial fires in Karnataka. Infrared thermography detects hidden hotspots weeks before ignition.
  • Karnataka Electrical Inspectorate Compliance: Meet the mandatory safety provisions enforced by the Karnataka Electrical Inspectorate under the Electricity Act, 2003 and CEA Regulations.
  • Protect IT & Data Infrastructure: For data centres and IT parks, even a momentary MCCB trip can cause massive data loss and downtime. Our predictive maintenance guarantees uptime.
  • BESCOM Grid Compliance: Ensure your internal electrical systems meet BESCOM standards for safe grid connectivity and avoid penalties.

Relevant Indian & International Standards

We ensure that your Bangalore facility passes both domestic and global scrutiny:

  • CEA Regulations (Safety Measures): The foundational national regulations for all electrical installations enforced by the Karnataka Electrical Inspectorate.
  • IS 3043: Code of practice for earthing — critical for protecting sensitive IT equipment and CNC machines.
  • IS 732: Code of practice for electrical wiring installations.
  • NFPA 70E: International best practices for workplace electrical safety and arc flash analysis.
  • NBC 2016: Ensuring fire-safe electrical routing and installations.

Our Proven 7-Step Process

1. Scope Verification: Reviewing existing Single Line Diagrams against physical plant modifications and BESCOM connection details.
2. Physical Clearances: Ensuring HT/LT rooms satisfy minimum statutory working clearances per CEA and Karnataka Inspectorate norms.
3. Thermography Scanning: Using Fluke/FLIR imagers to spot overheating contactors and loose connections in running panels without shutting down operations.
4. Earthing Verification: Testing earth resistance on sensitive utility equipment per IS 3043 standards.
5. Insulation & Protection Testing: Verifying insulation resistance of cables and calibrating protective relays and ELCBs/RCCBs.
6. Comprehensive Reporting: Providing a risk-categorization matrix highlighting immediate fire or shock hazards with corrective action plan.
7. Direct Resolution: Deploying our team to execute re-cabling, lug replacement, or busbar balancing for immediate risk mitigation.

Case Study: Peenya Electronics Manufacturing Unit

Challenge: Frequent MCCB tripping and intermittent power fluctuations causing production line stoppages and component damage at a major electronics assembly facility.

B2B Industrial Solution: Deployed thermography and power quality analysis. Identified severe 5th and 7th order harmonics from VFD-driven assembly lines feeding back into the LT panel, with neutral temperatures exceeding 110°C.

Results Delivered: Installed active harmonic filters, re-lugged critical connections, and upgraded earthing. Production uptime improved to 99.8% with zero unplanned trips.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Can you audit our facility in Peenya or Electronic City?

Absolutely. We deploy diagnostic thermography and electrical safety auditing teams to any industrial site across Bangalore, including Peenya, Electronic City, Whitefield, Bommasandra, and Jigani.

2. Does Karnataka mandate electrical safety audits?

Yes. The Karnataka Electrical Inspectorate enforces mandatory periodic inspections under the Electricity Act, 2003 and CEA Safety Regulations. Industrial installations must obtain Electrical Safety Clearance Certificates and maintain compliance for Fire NOC renewals.
