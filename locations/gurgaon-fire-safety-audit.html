What is a Fire Safety Audit in Gurgaon?

Gurgaon (Gurugram) is a massive industrial and corporate hub, where high-rise buildings and manufacturing plants require stringent fire safety measures. A fire safety audit in Gurgaon involves a thorough examination of a building's fire safety systems, including detection, suppression, and evacuation procedures, to ensure compliance with the National Building Code (NBC) 2016 and local Haryana Fire Services regulations.

Our Fire Audit Process

1. Document Review: Reviewing fire NOCs, maintenance logs, and previous audit reports.
2. Physical Inspection: Inspecting fire hydrants, sprinklers, extinguishers, and detection systems.
3. Egress Assessment: Verifying exit routes, signage, and emergency lighting.
4. Gap Analysis: Identifying deficiencies in existing fire safety infrastructure.
5. Detailed Report: Providing a comprehensive report with prioritized recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How often should a fire safety audit be conducted?

As per NBC guidelines, fire safety audits should be conducted at least once a year, or whenever significant changes are made to the building structure or occupancy.
