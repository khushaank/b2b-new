Built for Delhi operations

Delhi supports manufacturing units, hospitals, warehouses, institutional campuses and commercial facilities. These sites face dense mixed-use operations, ageing electrical infrastructure and narrow shutdown windows. A useful hse audit therefore needs more than a generic checklist: it must reflect how the facility actually runs, where access is restricted and which actions can be completed without disrupting production.

B2B Industrial Solutions plans fieldwork around operating conditions, gathers traceable evidence and presents findings in a format that engineering, EHS, finance and leadership teams can use together. The objective is worker health, operational safety and environmental discipline—with clear action ownership rather than a report that sits unread.

What the Delhi assessment covers

The final scope is confirmed after a short discovery call and document review. A typical engagement includes:

01

Workplace and machine safety

Guarding, access, material handling, work at height and contractor activity.

02

Permit and risk controls

HIRA, permit-to-work, LOTO, confined space and hot-work controls.

03

Occupational health

Exposure controls, PPE, ergonomics, welfare, first aid and health surveillance.

04

Management system

Training, inspections, incidents, emergency plans, records and leadership review.

A four-stage route from site visit to closeout

Define

Confirm objectives, site boundaries, operating shifts, records and access constraints.

Inspect

Review documents, interview responsible teams and assess live site conditions.

Prioritise

Grade findings by risk, urgency, evidence, effort and operational impact.

Close

Discuss actions, ownership and proof required to demonstrate completion.

Questions facility teams ask

How long does a hse audit take in Delhi?

Duration depends on area, process complexity, shifts and measurement scope. A focused single facility may need one or two field days; multi-block or multi-shift sites are planned in phases after document review.

Can the assessment happen while the facility is operating?

Yes, much of the review is more useful under representative operating load. Any intrusive test or isolation is agreed in advance and carried out only under the facility’s authorised safety procedure.

Does the report include implementation support?

Yes. Findings are discussed with responsible teams, and B2B Industrial Solutions can support scope definition, vendor coordination, engineering checks and closure verification where requested.
