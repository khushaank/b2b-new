Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is a BEE energy audit mandatory in Ahmedabad for all factories?

Mandatory audits depend on the annual energy consumption as per BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) guidelines for Designated Consumers. However, voluntary audits are highly recommended for Gujarat's textile and pharma hubs to stay competitive and reduce OH.

What is the typical ROI for energy audits in Sanand automotive units?

Automotive facilities often see a payback period of 6-12 months, primarily through compressed air leakage fixing and VFD installations on high-capacity motors.
