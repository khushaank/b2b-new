What is an Electrical Safety Audit in Noida?

Noida is home to massive industrial sectors and high-density IT hubs where electrical systems are under constant load. An electrical safety audit in Noida is a comprehensive review of your facility's electrical infrastructure to identify potential fire hazards, shock risks, and inefficiencies. Our audits ensure compliance with the Central Electricity Authority (CEA) regulations and Indian Electricity (IE) Rules.

Why Your Noida Industry Needs This Audit

  • Fire Prevention: Over 70% of industrial fires are electrical in origin.

Audit Scope

1. Visual Inspection: Reviewing SLDs, earthing, and cable management.
2. Thermography Study: Identifying hot spots in panels and transformers.
3. Testing & Measurement: Measuring earth resistance and insulation levels.
4. Power Quality: Analyzing harmonics and voltage fluctuations.
