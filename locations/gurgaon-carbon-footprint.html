What is a Carbon Footprint Analysis in Gurgaon?

Carbon footprint analysis is the process of calculating the total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions caused directly and indirectly by an organization. In Gurgaon, as corporate sustainability mandates (BRSR reporting) become stricter, analyzing and reporting carbon footprints is essential for businesses aiming for Net Zero goals and environmental leadership.

Our Carbon Footprint Analysis Scope

  • Scope 1 Emissions: Direct emissions from owned or controlled sources (e.g., fuel combustion, DG sets).
  • Scope 2 Emissions: Indirect emissions from the generation of purchased energy (electricity).
  • Scope 3 Emissions: All other indirect emissions in the value chain (e.g., travel, waste, procurement).
  • Emission Reduction Plan: Developing actionable strategies to lower your carbon intensity.
  • Certification Support: Assisting with carbon offset programs and international certifications.
