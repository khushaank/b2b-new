What is a Water Audit in Gurgaon?

A water audit is a systematic process of identifying, measuring, and optimizing water use in a facility. In Gurgaon, where water scarcity is a significant concern and groundwater levels are dropping, water audits are mandated by the Central Ground Water Authority (CGWA) for industries and commercial projects to ensure sustainable water management.

B2B Industrial Solutions provides BEE certified water auditing services in Gurgaon, helping facilities reduce water waste and comply with mandatory water conservation targets.

Why Your Gurgaon Industry Needs a Water Audit

  • CGWA Compliance: Fulfill the mandatory requirement for groundwater withdrawal permissions.
  • Cost Savings: Reduce utility bills by identifying leaks and optimizing process water.
  • Water Conservation: Implement recycling and reuse strategies (ZLD compliance).
  • Risk Mitigation: Ensure water security for long-term industrial operations.

Our 5-Step Water Audit Process

1. Water Flow Mapping: Identifying all water sources and distribution points.
2. Measurement & Monitoring: Using ultrasonic flow meters to record real-time water usage.
3. Water Balance Study: Comparing water input with output to identify unaccounted losses.
4. Conservation Opportunities: Identifying potential for rainwater harvesting and wastewater recycling.
5. Certification & Reporting: Providing a CGWA compliant water audit report.
