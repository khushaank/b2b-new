What is a Thermography Audit in Gurgaon?

A thermography audit (thermal imaging) is a non-destructive testing method that uses infrared cameras to detect heat patterns and temperature variations in electrical and mechanical systems. In Gurgaon's industrial zones like Manesar and Udyog Vihar, thermography audits are a vital part of predictive maintenance, helping to identify loose connections, overloaded circuits, and bearing failures before they lead to fire or breakdown.

B2B Industrial Solutions uses high-resolution FLIR cameras to conduct thermography audits in Gurgaon, providing clear visual evidence of potential faults.

Why Your Gurgaon Facility Needs Thermography

  • Fire Prevention: Identify "hot spots" in electrical panels that could trigger a fire.
  • Zero Downtime: The audit is conducted while your facility is under normal load (live).
  • Energy Efficiency: Detect heat leaks in furnaces, boilers, and steam lines.
  • Insurance Compliance: Many industrial insurers mandate annual thermography reports.

Our Thermography Audit Scope

1. Electrical Panels: LT/HT panels, transformers, and busbars.
2. Rotating Equipment: Motors, bearings, and belts.
3. Steam Systems: Insulation failures and steam trap monitoring.
4. Building Envelope: Moisture detection and insulation gaps.
