What is an HVAC Audit in Gurgaon?

An HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) audit is a comprehensive evaluation of a building's cooling and ventilation systems. In Gurgaon's corporate IT parks and industrial facilities, HVAC systems often account for more than 50% of the total energy consumption. An audit helps identify inefficiencies in chillers, AHUs, and ducting networks to reduce operational costs and improve comfort.

B2B Industrial Solutions conducts specialized HVAC audits in Gurgaon, using calibrated instruments to measure flow, temperature, and power consumption.

Our HVAC Audit Scope

  • Chiller Performance: Measuring IKW/TR and evaluating heat exchanger efficiency.
  • Air Balancing: Ensuring proper air distribution and indoor air quality (IAQ).
  • Duct Integrity: Identifying air leakage in supply and return ducting.
  • VFD Optimization: Evaluating the potential for variable frequency drives on fans and pumps.
  • Thermal Comfort: Analyzing temperature and humidity levels across various zones.

Why Gurgaon Offices Need HVAC Audits

With Gurgaon experiencing extreme summers, HVAC systems work at peak capacity for several months. Inefficiencies not only lead to high electricity bills but also put excessive strain on equipment, leading to frequent breakdowns. A professional audit ensures that your system is sized correctly and operating at its design efficiency.
