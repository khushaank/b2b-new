Certified Emission Compliance Testing

Stack Emission Testing is a regulatory requirement for industries operating boilers, DG sets, furnaces, and process equipment. Our NABL-accredited laboratory provides comprehensive emission analysis to ensure compliance with CPCB, SPCB, and industry-specific emission norms.

We conduct isokinetic sampling for Particulate Matter (PM), and measure gaseous pollutants including SO₂, NOₓ, CO, CO₂, and VOCs. All test reports are recognized by pollution control boards for consent renewals and environmental clearances.
