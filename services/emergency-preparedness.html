Be Ready for the Unexpected

An effective Emergency Preparedness & Response Plan (EPRP) is the difference between a controlled incident and a catastrophe. Facilities handling hazardous chemicals or operating at high risk must have a well-documented and practiced plan.

B2B Industrial Solutions assists industries in developing On-Site and Off-Site Emergency Plans as per the Factories Act, 1948 and MSIHC Rules, 1989. We ensure your team knows exactly what to do when seconds count.

Key Components of EPRP

Risk Assessment

Identifying potential hazards (Fire, Toxic Release, Explosion) and their impact zones using software modeling.

View HIRA

Communication Strategy

Defining clear channels for alerting employees, authorities, and neighboring communities during a crisis.

Protocols

Emergency Team

Structuring the Incident Control Team (ICT), Site Controller, and Fire Fighting Squad with defined roles.

Team Structure

Evacuation Plan

Mapping escape routes, assembly points, and medical aid centers for safe personnel evacuation.

EPRP vs Disaster Management Plan

Understanding the scope of your safety planning.

Feature EPRP (Emergency Plan) DMP (Disaster Plan)
Focus Immediate response within minutes Long-term recovery & rehabilitation
Scope Site-specific incidents (Fire, Leak) Large scale calamities (Flood, EQ)
Regulation Factories Act / MSIHC Rules Disaster Management Act, 2005
