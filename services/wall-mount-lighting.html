Design Considerations

  • Beam Angle Selection: Narrow (15-30°) for accent lighting, medium (40-60°) for general illumination, wide (90-120°) for flood coverage.
  • Color Temperature: Warm white (3000K) for hospitality/residential, neutral (4000K) for commercial, cool white (6000K) for security.
  • IP Rating: IP65 minimum for outdoor installations, IP67 for in-ground/step lights exposed to water ingress.
  • Control Options: Manual switches, PIR motion sensors, photocells for dusk-to-dawn, or DMX/DALI for programmable scenes.
