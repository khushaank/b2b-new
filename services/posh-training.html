Prevention of Sexual Harassment

The Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Act, 2013 mandates every organization with 10 or more employees to establish a safe and respectful workplace. B2B Industrial Solutions provides comprehensive POSH training, Internal Committee (IC) setup, and policy implementation to ensure legal compliance and foster a healthy work culture.

Our expert-led workshops raise awareness, educate employees on rights and responsibilities, and empower organizations to handle complaints effectively.
