First Response to Industrial Hazards

A fire extinguisher is the most effective tool for dousing a fire in its incipient stage. However, having the wrong type of extinguisher or a non-functional one can be as dangerous as having none at all.

B2B Industrial Solutions provides a full range of high-quality, ISI Marked (IS 15683) extinguishers. We also offer certified refilling and pressure testing services to ensure your equipment is always ready for immediate action.
