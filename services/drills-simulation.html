Testing Readiness Before It Counts

An Emergency Response Plan (ERP) is only as good as the people executing it. Theoretical knowledge fades during a crisis; muscle memory takes over.

B2B Industrial Solutions conducts realistic, scenario-based mock drills to test the effectiveness of your On-Site Emergency Plan. We don't just "tick the box"—we expose gaps in communication, response time, and equipment functionality so you can fix them before a real disaster strikes.

Drill Hierarchy (As Per NBC 2016)

We conduct drills at various levels of complexity to ensure comprehensive preparedness.

Level Drill Type Participants Objective
Level 1 Functional Drill Specific Departments / ERT Test specific equipment (e.g., Hydrant pressure) or team skills.
Level 2 Full-Scale Mock Drill Entire Facility Test total evacuation and inter-departmental coordination.
Level 3 Community / Off-Site Public & District Authorities Manage disasters affecting neighboring areas (e.g., Toxic Cloud).
