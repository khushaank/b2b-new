Lift License & Safety Certification

Operating elevators and escalators requires strict adherence to the State Lift Act and Rules. We provide comprehensive liaison services to obtain the mandatory Lift License, NOC, and safety certificates for industrial, commercial, and residential buildings.

Our Services Include

  • New Lift License: Facilitating the initial application and inspection process for newly installed lifts.
  • License Renewal: Managing the timely renewal of existing lift licenses to ensure uninterrupted operation.
  • Safety Inspections: Coordinating with government inspectors for mandatory safety audits and clearance.
  • Modification Approvals: Obtaining permissions for major repairs or alterations to existing lift systems.
