Running Your Factory Legally

Obtaining a Factory License and Municipal Corporation (MCD) approvals is the fundamental requirement for operating any manufacturing unit. Missing or expired licenses can result in hefty penalties, production shutdowns, and legal complications.

B2B Industrial Solutions provides end-to-end liaison services to navigate bureaucratic processes and secure all mandatory licenses from local municipal bodies. We handle paperwork, coordinate with department officers, and ensure timely renewals so you can focus on running your business.
