Turnkey Solar Power Solutions

Our Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) services cover the entire project lifecycle—from feasibility studies and design to installation, commissioning, and grid synchronization. We handle everything so you can focus on your core business while we deliver clean, renewable energy.

We specialize in rooftop solar for commercial buildings, ground-mounted systems for industries, and microgrid solutions with battery storage. Our track record includes successful DISCOM approvals, MNRE subsidy assistance, and reliable O&M contracts.
