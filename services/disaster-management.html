Resilience Against the Unthinkable

A Disaster Management Plan (DMP) goes beyond routine emergency procedures. It prepares your organization for catastrophic events—such as massive fires, toxic gas releases, earthquakes, or floods—that threaten the very existence of your facility and the safety of the surrounding community.

B2B Industrial Solutions helps you build a robust DMP that coordinates resources, defines command hierarchies, and ensures rapid recovery, minimizing loss of life and assets.

Emergency Plan vs. Disaster Plan

Understanding the difference is key to effective preparedness.

Feature On-Site Emergency Plan (SEP) Disaster Management Plan (DMP)
Scope Confined to Factory Premises Extends to Neighboring Areas & Public
Leadership Site Incident Controller Main Incident Controller / top Mgmt.
External Aid Usually Not Required immediately Heavy Reliance (Police, Fire Brigade, District Admin)
Focus Containment & Evacuation Survival, Mitigation & Recovery
