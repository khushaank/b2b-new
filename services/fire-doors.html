Compartmentalize Smoke & Flames

Passive fire protection is a critical element of building safety. Fire Rated Doors act as physical barriers that prevent the spread of smoke, toxic gases, and heat between different zones of a building, protecting escape routes and containing property damage.

B2B Industrial Solutions provides doors tested and certified to IS 3614 Part II, UL 10C, and BS 476 standards, ensuring they remain structurally sound under intense thermal stress.
