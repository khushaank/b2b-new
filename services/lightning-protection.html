Shielding Assets from Nature's Fury

Industrial facilities with tall structures are prime targets for lightning strikes. A direct strike can cause catastrophic fires, structural damage, and destroy sensitive electronics. Without proper protection, a single lightning event can result in millions in losses and weeks of downtime.

B2B Industrial Solutions designs and audits Lightning Protection Systems (LPS) per IEC 62305 standards to ensure grounding integrity, surge protection adequacy, and overall coverage effectiveness. We help you meet insurance requirements and protect both personnel and critical assets.
