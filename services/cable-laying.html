Powering Your Infrastructure with Precision

Cabling is the lifeline of any industrial facility. Improper laying or termination is the leading cause of electrical fires and power outages. B2B Industrial Solutions provides end-to-end cable management services, from excavation and trenching to the final high-voltage termination, ensuring your power infrastructure is robust, safe, and compliant.

HT & LT Specialist
Zero-Fault Installation
IEC Compliant

Whether it's underground trenching for a new plant or upgrading overhead cable trays for expansion, our certified teams handle complex cable routes with minimal disruption to your operations.

Our Installation Scope

Route Engineering

Strategic route planning to minimize cable length and voltage drop while avoiding physical obstructions.

Laying & Dressing

Expert laying on trays, ladders, or underground trenches with proper bending radii and dressing.

Termination & Jointing

Using certified Raychem/3M kits for HT/LT terminations, ensuring moisture-proof and secure connections.

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive Megger and Hi-Pot testing to verify insulation integrity before energization.
