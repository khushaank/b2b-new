Fostering a Healthy & Engaged Workforce

Employee Well-Being is directly linked to productivity, retention, and organizational success. Our Workplace Well-Being Audit evaluates your facility's physical environment, mental health support systems, ergonomic practices, and wellness culture to identify opportunities for improvement.

We assess Indoor Air Quality (IAQ), lighting, noise, thermal comfort, psychosocial factors, canteen hygiene, fitness amenities, and the prevalence of musculoskeletal disorders (MSDs). Our recommendations help you build a "Great Place to Work" that attracts and retains top talent.

Audit Components

Physical Environment

IAQ testing (CO₂, VOCs, particulates), lux level measurement, noise mapping, and thermal comfort assessment.

Standards

Psychosocial Factors

Work-life balance policies, stress management programs, mental health first aid, and employee assistance programs (EAP).

Programs

Health Amenities

Canteen hygiene audit, nutritional menu review, drinking water quality, first-aid readiness, and on-site medical facilities.

Checklist

Ergonomic Assessment

Workstation design, MSD prevalence surveys, REBA/RULA analysis, and equipment recommendations (adjustable chairs, footrests).
