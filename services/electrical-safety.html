Comply with CEA Regulations 2023

Electrical faults are the leading cause of industrial fires. The Central Electricity Authority (Measures relating to Safety and Electric Supply) Regulations, 2023 mandate regular safety audits for high-rise buildings, industries, and commercial complexes.

B2B Industrial Solutions conducts systematic examinations of your electrical systems. We identify potential hazards like overloading, loose connections, and defective earthing, ensuring your facility is safe and legally compliant.

Audit Methodology

Visual Inspection

Physical verification of cables, panels, and switchgear for signs of overheating, aging, or damage.

Check Hazards

Testing & Measurement

Testing of Earth Pit resistance, ELCB/RCCB tripping times, and Insulation Resistance (Megger Test).

Validate Health

Thermography

Using thermal cameras to detect hotspots in terminations and busbars invisible to the naked eye.

Thermal Scan

Compliance Check

Verifying adherence to National Building Code (NBC 2016) and Indian Electricity Rules.
