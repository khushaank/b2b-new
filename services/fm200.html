Core Features of Our Suppression Systems

FM 200 Clean Agent

Electrically non-conductive, safe for occupied spaces, and leaves no residue, drastically minimizing operational downtime and equipment damage.

Kitchen Suppression

Targeted suppression for grease/fat fires (Class K) using specialized wet chemical agents, engineered specifically for commercial cooking environments.

Instant Detection

Integrating highly sensitive heat and smoke detectors to automatically deploy the agent within seconds of fire initiation.

End-to-End Service

From hazard assessment and system design to installation, testing, and periodic refilling and maintenance.

Why Invest in Specialized Fire Suppression

Installing the right fire suppression systems provides numerous operational and financial benefits:

  • Minimal Downtime: Because FM 200 leaves no residue to clean up, operations can typically resume almost immediately following system discharge and ventilation.
  • Asset Protection: No water damage means your expensive servers, data centers, and electronics remain unharmed during a structural fire scenario.
  • Regulatory Compliance: Stay completely compliant with the latest local and national fire safety codes (NFPA) for commercial spaces and kitchens.
  • Space Efficient: FM 200 systems require significantly less storage space compared to traditional inert gas systems or water reservoirs.
