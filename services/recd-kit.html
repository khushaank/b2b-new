Advanced Emission Reduction Technology

We supply and install ARAI & ICAT approved Retrofitted Emission Control Devices (RECD) and Dual Fuel Kits (DFK). These devices are essential for older DG sets to meet the new CPCB IV+ and NGT emission norms.

Our kits achieve >70% reduction in Particulate Matter (PM) while maintaining engine performance, and DFKs enable diesel-gas hybrid operation for significant fuel cost savings.
