Optimizing Combustion & Heat Transfer

Industrial boilers are among the highest energy consumers in any facility. Even a minor 1% to 2% improvement in efficiency can translate into millions in annual fuel savings for large-scale operations.

At B2B Industrial Solutions, we conduct rigorous performance assessments using both Direct (Input-Output) and Indirect (Heat Loss) methods. Our specialized audits identify the root causes of energy waste—from excess air and radiation losses to scaling and improper blowdown practices.

Reduce Fuel Consumption Lower Carbon Emissions Extend Equipment Life

Comprehensive Testing Methodology

Our experts use calibrated instruments to profile every aspect of your boiler's operation:

Combustion Analysis

We optimize air-to-fuel ratios to ensure complete combustion, minimizing unburnt losses and harmful emissions.

Stack Temperature Profile

Monitoring flue gas exit temperatures to detect fouled heat transfer surfaces or heat recovery opportunities.

Blowdown Optimization

Assessment of TDS levels to balance water quality with the thermal energy lost during blowdown cycles.
