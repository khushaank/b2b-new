Starting Every Shift Safely

Tool Box Talks (TBT) are short, focused safety discussions conducted before work begins. These 5-15 minute pre-shift meetings keep safety top-of-mind, address daily hazards, and provide a platform for workers to voice concerns.

We help organizations design engaging, relevant TBT programs with industry-specific content libraries, facilitator training, and compliance documentation systems. Our approach ensures TBTs are interactive, not just readings—maximizing participation and retention.
