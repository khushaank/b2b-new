What is an Earthing Audit?

A resilient earthing (grounding) system is the absolute baseline of electrical safety. Without it, fault currents during a short circuit or lightning strike have nowhere to dissipate, causing them to travel indiscriminately through machine casings or human operators. An Earthing Audit scientifically tests the resistance capacity and connectivity of your entire ground pit network to ensure safety mechanisms will function when required.

B2B Industrial Solutions utilizes the highly precise Fall-of-Potential method and specialized 4-pole earth testers to verify that every chemical or copper earth pit in your facility satisfies the strict ohmic values dictated by the CEA.

Why Your Industry Needs an Earthing Audit Now

Over time, earth pits dry out, conductive chemicals dissipate, and connecting strips corrode. A pit that was previously 1.5 Ohms five years ago might read an incredibly dangerous 40 Ohms today.

  • Prevent Fatal Electric Shocks: Ensure that the fault-clearance path offers zero resistance, triggering MCBs rather than electrifying humans.
  • Protect PLC & CNC Electronics: Precision manufacturing equipment requires specific ground references. Poor earthing introduces "noise," scrambling expensive controller boards and data streams.
  • Lightning Protection System (LPS) Viability: Without verified interconnected ground grids, a lightning strike on your roof arrestors will arc violently across the building structure.

Relevant Indian & International Standards

  • IS 3043: Formidable Indian Code of Practice for Earthing and acceptable resistance bands.
  • IS/IEC 62305: Standard detailing Lightning Protection Systems.
  • CEA Regulations (Safety): Defines specific requirement parameters for transformer neutral grounding.

Our Proven 7-Step Earthing Process

1. Visual & Layout Inspection: Checking physical routing, color coding (green/yellow), and checking for broken copper flats.
2. Fall-of-Potential Testing: Deploying 3-pin/4-pin Megger testers explicitly into the soil to check isolated earth pit resistance.
3. Soil Resistivity Analytics: Essential for determining new pit sizing in rocky or sandy terrain profiles.
4. Grid Interconnectivity Verification: Measuring touch and step potentials, ensuring multiple pits communicate seamlessly.
5. Neutral & Body Earth Verification: Validating that IT systems, generator neutrals, and equipment body earths are distinctly and safely separated per IS codes.
6. Formal Reporting: Supplying the NABL certified measurement certificates required by ISO auditors and state factory inspectors.
7. Direct Revamp: If pits fail, our turnkey team provides immediate chemical re-charging or fresh boring of maintenance-free earthing electrodes.
