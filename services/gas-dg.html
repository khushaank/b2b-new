Diesel vs. Gas Generators

Choosing the right fuel type depends on runtime, environmental regulations, and operating costs.

Factor Diesel DG Natural Gas DG
Fuel Cost Higher per liter, widely available Lower per cubic meter, requires pipeline
Emissions Higher NOx, particulates (requires DPF) Cleaner, lower emissions
Maintenance Higher, oil changes every 400 hrs Lower, cleaner combustion
Runtime Excellent for prime power, long hours Best for continuous duty
