PTW Process Lifecycle

A proper PTW system ensures systematic authorization and closure of hazardous work.

Phase Activities Responsible
Request & Preparation Work scope defined, hazards identified, safety measures planned Work Supervisor
Assessment & Authorization Site inspection, precautions verified, permit issued Permit Issuer
Execution Work carried out as per PTW conditions, monitoring ongoing Permit Receiver
Closure & Handback Work completed, area inspected, permit closed and archived Safety Officer
