Why Upgrade to CPCB IV+?

The new norms represent a technological leap, not just a rule change. Here's how CPCB IV+ compares to the older CPCB II:

Parameter CPCB II (Old) CPCB IV+ (New)
NOx + HC Emissions High Limits ~90% Reduction
Technology Mechanical / Semi-Electronic CRDI + Electronic Control Unit (ECU)
After-Treatment None (Usually) EGR + DOC + SCR (AdBlue)
Response Time Slower Load Acceptance Faster Transient Response
