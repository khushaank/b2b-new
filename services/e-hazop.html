Systematic Electrical Risk Assessment

In complex electrical networks, a simple inspection isn't enough. E-HAZOP (Electrical Hazard and Operability Study) is a structured, team-based brainstorming technique customized from the process industry to identify deviations from design intent that could create hazards.

B2B Industrial Solutions facilitates rigorous E-HAZOP workshops to uncover hidden vulnerabilities in your power distribution system, preventing blackouts, equipment damage, and safety incidents.

The Methodology

Node Selection

Breaking down the electrical system into manageable logical sections (nodes) like HV Switchgear, Transformers, or UPS systems.

Methodology

Guidewords

Applying standard parameters (Voltage, Current, Frequency) against guidewords (More, Less, None, Reverse) to find deviations.

See Examples

Safeguard Evaluation

Assessing if existing protection (relays, interlocks) is sufficient to handle the identified deviation consequences.

Risk Matrix

Recommendations

Providing actionable engineering solutions to reduce risk to ALARP (As Low As Reasonably Practicable) levels.
