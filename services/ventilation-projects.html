Engineered Ventilation Solutions

Industrial Ventilation is critical for maintaining indoor air quality (IAQ), controlling temperature and humidity, and ensuring worker safety. Our turnkey solutions include general exhaust ventilation, local exhaust ventilation (LEV), fresh air makeup systems, and smoke extraction for factories, warehouses, and commercial buildings.

We design systems complying with Factory Act, NBC, ASHRAE, and ISHRAE standards, using CFD analysis to optimize airflow patterns and ensure code compliance. Our scope includes ductwork fabrication, fan installation, electrical integration, and Testing & Balancing (T&B) for verified performance.
