Ensuring Safe Evacuation Under All Conditions

Life safety code compliance focuses on the human element during a fire emergency. It ensures that occupants can evacuate a building swiftly and safely, even when panic levels are high and visibility is low.

B2B Industrial Solutions conducts specialized Life Safety Audits that audit your facility against NBC 2016 Part 4 and NFPA 101 standards, focusing on exit capacities, travel distances, and emergency illumination.
