Statutory Testing (Factory Act)

We conduct rigorous testing to ensure your equipment meets safety standards defined in The Factories Act, 1948.

Form No. Testing Service
Form 13 Examination of Pressure Vessels (Boilers, Air Receivers) via Hydro-test & UT Thickness test.
Form 11 Testing and examination of Hoists, Lifts, Cranes, and Lifting Tackles/Tools.
Form 10 Report of examination of dust, fume, and other impurity extraction systems.
NDT Non-Destructive Testing (Ultrasonic, MPI, DPI) to detect internal flaws without damage.
