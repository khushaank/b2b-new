FAQ

Common Questions About Our Services

Concise answers about scope, delivery and proposal requirements.

Ask our technical team →
What industrial audits and engineering services do you provide?+

We provide technical studies, electrical and fire safety, statutory compliance support, HVAC and ventilation, sustainability and emission control, and turnkey project execution.

Do you only conduct audits, or do you also execute the recommendations?+

We also support engineering design, procurement, installation, rectification, testing and commissioning.

Can you support Fire NOC, Electrical NOC and other approvals?+

Yes. We support technical documentation, assessments and coordination for applicable Fire, Electrical, Lift, MCD and environmental approvals.

Do you undertake complete HVAC and ventilation projects?+

Yes. Scope can include design, equipment selection, installation, ventilation, air balancing, indoor-air-quality assessment, testing and commissioning.

Do you undertake complete fire-protection projects?+

Yes. Our scope can include fire-risk assessment, system design, detection, suppression, installation, testing and emergency preparedness support.

Do you provide services across India?+

Yes. We support facilities across India through on-site assessments, regional project coordination and multi-location delivery.

What information is required to receive a proposal?+

Share the facility location, industry, required service, site size or system capacity, current concern and preferred timeline.
